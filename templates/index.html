Input Addresses

Let us know where you and your friends are! If you don't have any friends, check out: I Don't Have Friends. But if you're like us and you have a life, sit back, relax, and let the hard part of meeting up happen for you.

Add a place type

Whether you want to go to a wax museum, reptile shop, or breakdancing studio, we have you covered. We won't judge you for any of your requests. Tell us how far from the central point you'd be willing to go (up to 30 miles).

Go on your adventure

Destinations will be outputted to specifically fit your desires, leaving behind the struggles of picking a restaurant for your date. We've covered the most difficult part of the rendezvous, but only YOU can get up off your couch and hit the road.

Our Mission

Here at Rendezvous, we know that meeting up with your coworkers, friends, and family can be a hassle. It's hard to pick a central point to meet up at, and even harder to pick a place to go. Our mission is to get you and your friends to the places most suited for you, without wasting your time, gas or money. Too long has meeting up been an inconvenience. With Rendezvous, meeting up is a transformative experience.
